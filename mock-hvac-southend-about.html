About SouthEnd Climate Co.

Family-owned. Charlotte-grown. Here for your comfort.

We started SouthEnd Climate Co. over twelve years ago with one goal: treat every home like it's our own. That means upfront pricing, no surprise fees, and respect for your time. We're a family-owned company serving Charlotte and the surrounding areas—South End, NoDa, Plaza Midwood, Dilworth, and beyond.

Our mission is simple: transparency and reliability. You deserve to know what you're paying before we show up. You deserve reminders and a clear cancellation policy—not a runaround. That's why we use Venu+ for booking, payments, and customer communication. You get a modern experience; we get to focus on your HVAC.
