About Peachtree Comfort HVAC

Peachtree Comfort HVAC is a family-owned and operated company that has been serving Atlanta and the metro area for the past 15 years. We’re not a big corporate chain—we’re your neighbors, and we take pride in treating every home and business like our own.

Our team is licensed, insured, and trained on the latest equipment, but what matters most to us is doing the job right and standing behind our work. We believe in honest pricing, clear communication, and showing up when we say we will. Whether you need a repair, a new system, or regular maintenance, you can count on us to be straightforward and reliable.

We’re here for the long haul—for your next emergency fix, your seasonal tune-up, or the day you’re ready for a new installation. Give us a call or book online whenever you’re ready. We’d be glad to help.
