About Lakefront Electric

Our Story

Lakefront Electric was founded in 2010 by Master Electrician Andre Washington. What started as a one-van operation on Chicago's North Side has grown into a trusted team handling residential and commercial work across the city and North Shore.

We're locally owned, we follow code on every job, and we answer the phone 24/7 because electrical emergencies don't wait.
